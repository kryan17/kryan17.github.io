Harvey's page

Harvey is a special character- as you see below, he was out of town when Grandpa was murdered and so he is not a suspect. As the doctor, he is the town coroner and so if he feels comfortable with you, he might be able to share more details about Grandpa’s demise.


Where were you when Grandpa died?

I was out of town at a medical conference in Calico desert – I heard an interesting talk about the healing properties of proper nutrition.

Can you tell me more about how you think Grandpa died?

Your Grandpa was NOT drowned – his lungs are clear so that is not the cause of death.
Requires you to be good friends.

Can you tell me more about Grandpa's cause of death?

Your Grandpa was NOT bludgeoned – blunt force trauma is not the cause of death.
Requires you to be close friends

Can you tell me even more about Grandpa's cause of death?

Your Grandpa was NOT electrocuted – that is not the cause of death.
Requires you to be close friends

Can you tell me yet more about Grandpa's cause of death?

Your Grandpa was stabbed or cut to death.
Requires you to be close friends

Can you tell what Grandpa's cause of death WAS please?

Your Grandpa was posioned. So sorry for your loss.
Requires you to be close friends
